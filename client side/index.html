Who We Are

Welcome to Young People’s Network International (TYPNI)—a global movement of passionate, driven, and visionary young people who are shaping a better world. We are a platform that brings together youth from across borders to collaborate, lead, and thrive. Whether you're a changemaker, student, artist, entrepreneur, or advocate, YPNI is here to amplify your voice, support your growth, and connect you with like-minded peers and mentors.

Our Initiatives

Young People’s Network International (YPNI) is a youth-led, youth-focused movement that connects, equips, and elevates young people to lead positive change across borders. We work to build a world where young people have the agency, tools, and support they need to shape their own futures—and their communities.
