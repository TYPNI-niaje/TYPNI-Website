About Us

Who We Are

The Young People's Network International (TYPNI) is a global initiative dedicated to creating a world where young people are empowered, connected, and supported in their journey of fulfillment. We bring together youth from diverse backgrounds, equipping them with resources, knowledge, and networks that foster growth, leadership, and impact in their spheres of influence

The Network is built on the belief that youth are the key drivers of change and when provided with the right opportunities, they can shape a better, more unified world. Through mentorship, innovation and collaboration, TYPNI is a bridge between ambition and achievement.
