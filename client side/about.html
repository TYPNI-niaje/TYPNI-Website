At TYPNI, we believe in the power of youth to shape the future.

The Young People's Network International (TYPNI) is membership based, youth-led, and youth-centered registered NGO that was founded on 28th September 2023 to provide an inclusive environment for the young people to connect, grow, and engage productively on significant aspects affecting their lives and that of their community. Our mission is to connect and empower young people globally through dynamic initiatives and programs that foster collective growth and positive transformation. We envision an empowered, vibrant community of young people, connected to endless opportunities for oneness and progress. We are committed to fostering a culture of accountability, originality, empowerment, and belonging.
